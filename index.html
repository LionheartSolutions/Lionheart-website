How It Works

What to Expect
From an FFL Transfer

Never done a transfer before? It's simpler than you think. Here's the process from purchase to pickup.

01
Purchase Online

Find the firearm you want on any licensed retailer: GunBroker, Bud's Gun Shop, Brownells, Palmetto State Armory, or anywhere else. Purchase it just like any online order.

02
Ship to Lionheart

At checkout, select "ship to FFL" and provide our license information. The retailer ships directly to us. You'll receive a notification once your firearm arrives. Contact us for our FFL info prior to ordering.

03
Pick Up Your Firearm

Schedule an appointment, bring a valid government-issued ID, complete the ATF Form 4473, and pass the standard NICS background check. Once cleared, your firearm is yours. Total transfer fee: $30 flat.

Questions about the process? Contact us before you order.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is an FFL transfer and how does it work?

An FFL transfer allows you to legally receive a firearm purchased online or from an out-of-state dealer. The seller ships directly to Lionheart Defense, we conduct the required ATF Form 4473 and NICS background check, then you pick up your firearm. It's the safe, legal way to buy guns online and we make the process straightforward.

Do you offer concealed carry (CCW) or defensive shooting training?

Yes. We offer professional firearms training for all experience levels, including defensive shooting fundamentals and CCW preparation. Training is conducted by a qualified instructor with a commitment to both safety and confident, responsible ownership.

Can you order a specific firearm or accessory I can't find locally?

Absolutely. Through our nationwide distributor network we can special order the vast majority of firearms, optics, and accessories. Contact us with the make, model, and configuration you're looking for and we'll do the legwork.
